# 🎥 SecValid AI YouTube Video Production Guide

This folder contains all the assets and scripts you need to generate a professional video for your YouTube channel.

## 📁 Assets Included
1.  **`video-preview.html`**: An interactive web-based video presentation. It includes all scenes, transitions, and animations.
2.  **`voiceover-script.json`**: The complete script with Scene numbers, Voiceover text, and Subtitles.
3.  **`video-script.json`**: A technical breakdown of the scene durations.

## 🚀 How to Generate the Video

### Step 1: Record the Visuals
1.  Open `video-preview.html` in your browser (Chrome or Edge recommended).
2.  Set your browser zoom to **100%** or adjust the `transform: scale(0.6)` in the file to `scale(1.0)` if you have a 4K monitor.
3.  Use **OBS Studio** or any screen recorder to record the browser window.
4.  Click the **"Play Video Sequence"** button on the bottom right.
5.  Wait for the sequence to finish.

### Step 2: Generate AI Voiceover
1.  Open `voiceover-script.json`.
2.  Copy the `voiceover` text for each scene.
3.  Use an AI voice generator like **ElevenLabs**, **Murf.ai**, or **OpenAI TTS**.
4.  Choose a "Professional Tech Narrator" or "Futuristic" voice profile.
5.  Export the audio files.

### Step 3: Combine in Video Editor
1.  Import your screen recording and the AI audio files into a video editor (CapCut, Premiere Pro, or DaVinci Resolve).
2.  Align the audio with the transitions in the video.
3.  (Optional) Add background music (Cyber-ambient or Synthwave works best).
4.  Export in **1080p** or **4K**.

## 🎨 YouTube Thumbnail Tip
Use the **final scene** of the video or the **Risk Score Gauge** (Scene 6) as the base for your thumbnail. Add some bright "Glow" effects and the text "AI vs HACKERS" for high CTR!
