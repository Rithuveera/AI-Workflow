**Body**:
A few weeks ago, I shared our initial **n8n + Playwright** workflow integrated with Slack. It was a great start—but great isn't enough for enterprise-scale reliability.

Today, I’m excited to showcase the **Technical Upgrade** of our High-Fidelity Automation Hub. We’ve migrated our entire communication loop to **Google Chat & Gmail** to provide a more integrated, high-transparency feedback system.

**What’s New?**
✅ **Omnichannel Reporting**: Real-time per-test results are now pushed directly to **Google Chat**, keeping the team in sync without leaving their workspace.
✅ **Precision Triage via Gmail**: When a regression occurs, the system doesn't just send a text alert. You get a high-priority **Gmail notification** containing:
   - Full Error Traces
   - Technical Logs
   - **Automated Issue Screenshots** attached for instant visual proof.
✅ **Executive Summaries**: A beautifully formatted **HTML Summary Report** is dispatched via Gmail at the end of every suite, providing a 360-degree view of your product health.

**Technical Core**:
- **Dual-Triggering**: Manual on-demand suites + Scheduled health checks.
- **Dynamic Discovery**: Automatically identifies and runs new `.spec.js` files.
- **Self-Healing Architecture**: Zero-manual intervention from trigger to final report.

This isn't just a notification update—it's a complete shift toward **Visual Traceability** and **Autonomous Quality Engineering**.
